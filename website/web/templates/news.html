About Sentiment Analysis

FinBERT is a pre-trained NLP model specifically designed for financial sentiment analysis. It analyzes news articles and classifies them into:

Positive — Optimistic outlook, price increases, favorable market conditions
Negative — Pessimistic outlook, price drops, unfavorable market conditions
Neutral — Balanced or factual information without clear sentiment direction
