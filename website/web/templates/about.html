Project Overview

This web application is a comprehensive system for predicting Crude Palm Oil (CPO) commodity prices using advanced machine learning techniques. The system integrates Hidden Markov Models (HMM) for market state prediction and FinBERT for news sentiment analysis.

The goal is to provide traders and analysts with a robust tool that not only forecasts prices but also identifies the underlying market regime (Bullish, Bearish, or Neutral) to support better decision-making.

Methodology

Hidden Markov Model

Used to model the hidden states of the market. It assumes that the market is always in one of several states (Bullish, Bearish, Neutral) which are not directly observable but can be inferred from price movements.

FinBERT Sentiment

A BERT model pre-trained on financial texts. It analyzes news headlines and snippets to determine market sentiment, providing an additional layer of insight beyond technical analysis.
